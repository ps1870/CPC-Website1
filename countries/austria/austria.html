Core Values

Slogan: “Spirt in Motion”

  • Paralympic athletes will perform at their best through power, motivation and concentration.
Current Challenge:
  • Financing and organizing delegations’ participation.

Focus

The goals of the ÖPC go beyond organization and financing. There are three main pillars:

  • Core competence: Planning, preparation and organization of the Austrian delegation, events promoting Paralympic youth sport and budgeting.
  • Awareness raising: Creating public attention on the Paralympic games by increasing media awareness and networking.
  • Advocacy: Representing Austria’s interests in general assembles and conferences of the International Paralympic Committee (IPC).
    • Encouraging inclusion and equality.
    • Close collaboration with the IPC and the European Paralympic Committee (EPC).

Austrian Paralympic Committee Mission and Goals

  • Goals of the Austrian Paralympic committee are based on three pillars one of which is Raising Awareness
  • Under this pilar - a focus on athletes, commitment to safety and respect in sport, fair play and anti-doping are directly listed as goals

External

Relationship with NOC

According to the Austrian Paralympic Committee website, the Austrian Olympic Committee is not listed as a partner.

Leading up to London 2012, the Austrian Paralympic Committee invited top Olympian and Paralympian athletes to participate in various Paralympic events at Schmelz University Sports Centre in Vienna, which was supported by NPC Austria’s sponsors, the lottery, and Bank Austria. This event educated able-bodied participants on the difficulties Paralympians face to participate in their sports and showcased a variety of Para-sports.
